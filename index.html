Lets Play!!

Tutorial

Objective of the game

To result 100 points before your opponent

The Rules

  1. 2 ones are called snake eyes if you get them you lose your points for the whole game once I lost them at 96
  2. Roll on every turn
  3. Mark all your points on a piece of paper
  4. Roll 1 or more time until you want to
  5. if you get a one in any of your rolls you get a 0 for that round
  6. Miss a turn if you got Snake eyes

Playing the game

  1. First roll your dice
  2. Add the result you got,If you got a 1 your turn is over and put 0 for your

    result

  3. Roll again if you want to (Rolls as many times as you want if you feel you are lucky)
  4. When you are done the opponent does the same thing
  5. If you or your opponent gets snake eyes you miss a turn and lose all your points
  6. Mark all your points on a piece of paper even your opponents
  7. And don't forget to sum all your points for a round and when done for a game

How to win

Have 100 points before your opponent
